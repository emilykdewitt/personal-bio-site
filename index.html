Hello there! I'm Emily. I'm a full-stack web development student at Nashville Software School. I grew up in Hartwell, Georgia, a small town in northeast Georgia. I earned a Bachelor of Science in Spanish and International Health from Clemson University and a Master of Arts in Teaching from Marian University.

After graduating from Clemson, I was a teacher and administrator in Indianapolis Public Schools for 3 years. I moved to Nashville in 2015 to launch a new computer science charter school, which is when I was bit by the computer science bug. I was blown away by our students' achievements and the opportunities that our computer science program offered them.

I currently work with slightly older students as the Director of Operations at Chi Psi Fraternity and plan to use my web development skills in conjunction with my operations expertise to provide clients with simple, effective, and powerful web solutions.
